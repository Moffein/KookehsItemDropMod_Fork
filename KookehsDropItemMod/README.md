### Kookeh's original DropItemMod ported to BepInEx and R2API!

Allows players to drop their own items out of their inventories to trade with friends / throw away items!

### Requirements:

- \>= R2API 2.5.7
- \>= MiniRpcLib 0.0.2
- \>= BepInExPack 5.3.1
